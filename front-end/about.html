Penny for your thoughts

The mission of Penny


Staying connected whilst staying apart


Penny is a different type of chat app. Its one that wants to bridge the gap of staying connected through social isolation. Penny you to express yourself through images, colours and words. So even if you feel you have nothing to say, you can still let someone know you are thinking about them. We want people to feel together in spirit. Our name is based on the idiom 'A penny for your thoughts' a classic british saying of wanting to know what you are thinking during silence.
